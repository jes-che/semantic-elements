Semantic Elements

What are Semantic Elements?

Summary:

Semantic elements are elements with a meaning; they clearly describe their meaning in a human- and machine-readable way. There are several benefits of using semantic elements. First, it has greater accessibility. Not only we humans find that semantic elements are easier to understand, assistive technologies are also able to better read and interpret the webpages. Next, it helps computers make sense of our content and increases searchability. Semantic elements also enhance internationalization since only 13% of the world are English native speakers. Last but not least, using a semantic element enhances interoperability as it helps other porgrammers understand the structure of our webpages.
